This online course was design by 5 students from Systems Engineering and Multimedia Engineering from the University San Buenaventura Cali - Colombia, oriented by professor Walter Germán Magaña Sandoval. It was created to fulfill the need to implement and share the knowledge obtained up until now, also to offer a work guide for future generations that would allow them to learn in a dynamic way and so it can be a support guide for the professor.
